BOS Board

At BOS Philly, our mission is to bring the excitement and energy of circuit parties back to Philadelphia while also supporting local LGBT charities. To achieve this, we produce large-scale events featuring top-notch production values and international DJs, who bring the best music that Philadelphia has ever seen. These events are held at stunning venues throughout the city, creating an unforgettable experience for attendees.

But we don’t just throw parties for the fun of it – we also use these events as an opportunity to give back to the local community. We believe it is important to support the LGBT charities that do so much for us throughout the year, and by attending our events, you can have a great time while also making a positive impact on the community.

Our events are more than just a night out – they are truly one-of-a-kind experiences that you won’t find anywhere else. From the music to the production values, we strive to create an atmosphere that is both fun and inclusive. And with the added bonus of supporting local charities, you can feel good about supporting a worthy cause while you dance the night away.

So come join us at one of our events and be a part of something special. Together, we can make a difference in the lives of those around us while having a great time doing it.
